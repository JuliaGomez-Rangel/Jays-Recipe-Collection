## Ingredients
- 1 1/2 pounds chicken thighs or breast (whole or chopped)
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 2 teaspoons ginger paste
- 2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce
- 3 tablespoons green curry paste
- 2 teaspoons coriander
- 1/4 cup coconut milk
- 1/2 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
- 1 tablespoon chopped basil
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
## Instructions
1. In a bowl, whisk together coconut oil, garlic, ginger paste, soy sauce, green curry paste, coriander, coconut milk, salt, pepper, brown sugar, cilantro, basil, and lime juice.
2. Add chicken and mix until every piece is coated.
3. Transfer the chicken and marinade to a freezer-safe bag or container, press out excess air, and seal.
4. Label and freeze for up to 3 months.
5. To thaw: Place in the fridge overnight, or submerge sealed bag in cold water for a quicker thaw (about 1â€“2 hours).
6. Cook in a pan until caramelized and cooked through, or air fry for a crisp finish.
