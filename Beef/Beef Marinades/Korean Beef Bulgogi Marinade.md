## Ingredients
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 2 tablespoons mirin
- 6 cloves garlic
- 1 green onion
- 1/2 kiwi
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 2 teaspoons sesame seeds
## Instructions
1. Combine all the marinade ingredients together in a medium sized bowl and stir until combined.
